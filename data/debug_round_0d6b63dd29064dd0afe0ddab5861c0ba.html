Black Sabbath released their album Paranoid in September 1970, and the entire album is filled with absolute legendary songs, from this track to the title song to Iron Man to Fairies Wear Boots.

This song was of course written in the midst of the Vietnam War, but its message is sadly still relevant today. If only the War Pigs of today would give up their fatal game.
